About Us

Welcome to Vivid Memories Photography! I'm Ben Rice, and I'm passionate about capturing the magical moments that tell your story. My goal is to create images that are not just seen, but deeply felt, preserving lasting memories for you and your loved ones.

The most cherished photographs come from a place of comfort and authenticity. That's why I focus on capturing genuine emotions, celebrating unique bonds, and preserving the true joy in every individual story. My aim is always to create a relaxed and authentic environment for every person we work with.
