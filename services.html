The Vivid Memories Experience

1

Before Your Session

After you contact us, your journey begins as we discuss your vision, get to know you and the style of photography you’re looking for, and plan the perfect location that reflects that style. We provide guidance on timing to ensure we get the most beautiful light for your photos and attire to complement your vision and ensure you look and feel your absolute best for your photoshoot.

2

During Your Photoshoot

During your photoshoot, our primary aim is for you to feel comfortable and relaxed. We create a fun, laid-back atmosphere, guiding you through natural poses and prompts that encourage genuine interaction and emotion, all while capturing you in beautiful light and artful composition.

3

After Your Session

After our session, we’ll meticulously select the best images and hand-edit each one to bring out its vibrancy and warmth. You can expect your final gallery within a few days to a week, though some larger sessions may take up to 2-3 weeks. Before we deliver your stunning images, we'll ask you for payment, and to sign a standard release. Once the release is signed and payment is received, your high-resolution images will be delivered via a secure cloud storage link for printing, and a private Google Photos album for convenient social media and mobile sharing.
